Hamburgers (⏰: 20 minutes)

← Back to Recipe Index 🍽

Ingredients:

  • 800g Extra Lean Ground Beef
  • 1 Egg
  • 1/3 Cup Rolled Oats
  • 1/4 Tsp Salt
  • 1/4 Tsp Pepper

Directions:

  1. Add all ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. Mix with your hands, squeezing the meat between your fingers.
  3. Form into 6 patties. Patties should be slightly thinner in the middle than on the outside.
  4. Cook on the BBQ or in a grill (10 minutes).
  5. Top with your favourite toppings and put on your favourite bun. Enjoy!

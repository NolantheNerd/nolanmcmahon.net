Vegetable Lasagna (⏰: 2-3 Hours)

← Back to Recipe Index 🍽

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 Cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 Small/Medium Red Pepper
  • 1 Small/Medium Carrot
  • 1 Small Onion
  • 3 Roma Tomatoes
  • 3/4 Cup White Mushrooms
  • 1 Small Zucchini
  • 3 Cloves of Minced Garlic
  • 12 Ounces Tomato Paste
  • 1 Cup Chicken Broth
  • 2 Tbsp Brown Sugar
  • 2 Tsp Oregano
  • 2 Tsp Basil
  • 1 Tsp Salt
  • 1/2 Tsp Thyme
  • 1/4 Tsp Pepper
  • 9 Lasagna Noodles
  • 2 Medium/Large Eggs
  • 2 Cups Light Ricotta Cheese
  • 1 Cup Mozzarella Cheese
  • 1/3 Cup Parmesan Cheese
  • 2 Tsp Italian Seasoning

Preparation:

  1. Wash and Julienne (Cut into Long Strips) the Red Pepper
  2. Peel, Wash, and Shred the Carrot with a Cheese Grater
  3. Dice the Onion
  4. Set the Pepper, Carrot, and Onion Aside
  5. Wash and Slice the Tomatoes into Small Half Slices
  6. Wash and Slice the Mushrooms
  7. Wash, Remove Ends, and Slice the Zucchini into Thick Slices, Cut Slices in Half
  8. Set Tomatoes, Mushrooms, and Zucchini Aside
  9. In a Small Bowl Mix the Brown Sugar, Oregano, Basil, Salt, Thyme, Pepper and Set Aside
  10. Grate the Mozzarella Cheese and Parmesan Cheese and Combine, Set Aside
  11. In a Small Bowl, Add Eggs and Lightly Beat
  12. Add Ricotta Cheese to the Eggs and Combine, Set Aside

Directions:

  1. In large pot, heat olive oil over medium-high heat (1-2 minutes)
  2. Add red pepper, carrot, and onion. Cook and stir periodically until crisp-tender. (5-10 minutes)
  3. Add tomatoes, mushrooms, zucchini, and minced garlic. Cook and stir periodically until zucchini is crisp-tender. (7-15 minutes)
  4. Add tomato paste, broth, and spice mixture. Bring to a boil.
  5. Once the mixture reaches a boil, reduce temperature to low/simmer and cook for 30 minutes, uncovered. Stir occasionally.
  6. Preheat oven to 350°F. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions and set aside.
  7. In a large, glass baking dish, construct the lasagna layers as follows:
    • 1/3 of the Vegetable Mixture
    • 3 Lasagna Noodles
    • 1/2 of the Ricotta Cheese Mixture
    • 1/3 of the Vegetable Mixture
    • 3 Lasagna Noodles
    • 1/2 of the Ricotta Cheese Mixture
    • 1/3 of the Vegetable Mixture
    • 3 Lasagne Noodles
    • All the Mozzarella and Parmesan Cheese
    • The Italian Seasoning
  8. Bake for 30-35 minutes, uncovered.
  9. Let stand for 5 minutes.
  10. Enjoy!

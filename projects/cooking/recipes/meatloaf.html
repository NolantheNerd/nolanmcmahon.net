Meatloaf (⏰: 1 Hour)

← Back to Recipe Index 🍽

Ingredients:

  • 800g Extra Lean Ground Beef
  • 1/4 Cup Onion
  • 15 Unsalted Saltine Crackers
  • 1 Egg
  • 3 Tbsp Light Cheez Whiz
  • 4 Tbsp BBQ Sauce

Preparation:

  1. Line a Metalic Baking Tray with Aluminium Foil
  2. Dice or Blend the Onion
  3. Crush the Crackers
  4. Preheat Oven to 375°F

Directions:

  1. In a large bowl, add beef, onion, egg, and Cheez Whiz. Mix with your hands, squeezing the meat through your fingers.
  2. Transfer to baking tray and flatten into an inch or so thick loaf.
  3. Spread BBQ sauce on top of the loaf as desired.
  4. Bake at 375°F for 45 minutes.
  5. Slice and serve. Enjoy!

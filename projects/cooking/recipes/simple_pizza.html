Simple Pizza (⏰: 1 Hour)

← Back to Recipe Index 🍽

Ingredients:

  • 6 Whole Wheat Naan
  • 2 Cans (430mL) Pizza Sauce
  • 1/2 Block (350g) Mozzarella Cheese
  • 200g of Deli Pepperoni OR 200g of Black Forest Ham
  • 6 White Mushrooms OR 1 Can (400mL) of Pineapple Tidbits

Preparation:

  1. Shred Cheese
  2. Slice Mushrooms or Drain Pineapple Tidbits
  3. Preheat oven to 375°F
  4. Line Baking Sheet with Aluminium Foil

Directions:

  1. Spread pizza sauce on naan.
  2. Distribute pepperoni/ham over the pizzas.
  3. Spread the mushrooms/pineapple tidbits over the pizzas.
  4. Top each pizza with cheese.
  5. Bake for 15 minutes.
  6. Enjoy!

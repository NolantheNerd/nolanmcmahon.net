Waffles (⏰: 20 minutes)

← Back to Recipe Index 🍽

Ingredients:

  • 2 Cups 1% Milk
  • 1/2 Cup Butter or Margarine
  • 1 Tbsp Sugar
  • 1/2 Tsp Salt
  • 4 Tsp Baking Powder
  • 3 Cups Flour

Directions:

  1. Combine ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. Mix with an electric mixer (or elbow grease).
  3. Pour an appropriate amount in your waffle maker of choice.
  4. Top with toppings of your choice and enjoy!

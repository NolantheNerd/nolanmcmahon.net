Broccoli Stir-Fry (⏰: 30-90 minutes)

← Back to Recipe Index 🍽

Ingredients:

  • 2 Chicken Breasts
  • 1 1/4 Cups of Kraft Asian Sesame Dressing
  • 5 Tbsp of Soy Sauce
  • 1/4 Tsp of Garlic Powder
  • 1 Tsp Ground Ginger
  • 1 Box (375g) Fettuccini Noodles
  • 1 Large Crown of Broccoli

Preparation:

  1. Wash and Cut Broccoli into Florets

Directions:

  1. Cut chicken breasts into small pieces and add to a pan. Add pepper if desired. Cook chicken on medium heat until it is no longer pink. (10-15 minutes)
  2. Meanwhile in a bowl add dressing, soy sauce, and spices. Whisk until homogeneous.
  3. Once chicken is cooked, add sauce and turn heat down to low. Simmer for up to an hour, stirring occasionally.
  4. Cook fettuccini noodles according to package directions. When fettuccini is 2-3 minutes from fully cooked, add broccoli to the noodles and stir.
  5. Add chicken and sauce to noodles and broccoli and mix.
  6. Enjoy!
